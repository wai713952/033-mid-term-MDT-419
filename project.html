Hounted Goose | 2020

Hounted Goose is a retro-graphic style horror game made by Unity. The player needs to run away from the goose-like monster that haunted the abandoned factory. My role in making this game is creating the story, game event, 3D models, scene decoration, monster’s animation, and soundtrack. There is also an enhanced graphic trailer of this video game. Hounted Goose full version can be download for free at GameJolt.com or watch the gameplay video on YouTube by searching the name.

Demon Conquest | 2020

An RPG dice roller adventure board game. Inspired by Zombicide and Dark Souls game series. To win the game, players must make their way to the final boss of the game as fast as possible. Four players have to choose their character that comes with different playstyle and abilities. The players will level up by gaining the experience and crystal(currency) through monster killing. Find equipment from loot chests in the map or purchase from the merchant, use the consumable item to make the fight easier. Rolling the 8-faces dice will involve tiles player can travel, monster appearance, battle, and character’s ability.

Metal Breaker | 2021

A pixel side-scrolling action roguelike mobile game. The core gameplay was inspired by Mega Man and Hades. Player has to fight the enemies and pass the stage to boss. Each run will provide the new play style, the skill player gets while playing in the stage will be random, to create dynamic gameplay like many rogue-like games. The animation of the characters and effect are pixel sprite base. Metal Breaker is available for download on Google Play Store.
